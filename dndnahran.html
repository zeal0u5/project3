Nahran Umbra

Summary and Background

As a young child, Nahran lived with his parents on the Limbo plane. While living in Limbo, there was an uprising and somehow Nahran was sent to the material plane alon. He was found by his soon to be mentor, Ryo Enclava, and taken to Lathander's Light Monestary to live, grow up, and learn. While growing up he devoted everything he could to learning and training. Now, as a 23 year old, he has decided to leave the monestary since Ryo had found a new mentee. Nahran is now learning about the outside world and fueling his passion of plants and nature. He is trying his best to fit in and wants to, above all else, not let anyone down.
